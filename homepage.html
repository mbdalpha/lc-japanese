Japanese in the Leaving Certificate is a not often taken yet quite approachable language, especially as it expects zero knowledge going into 5th year and thus is assessed to a lower level than other languages such as French, Spanish, and German. It is a very good language to take if you have an interest in Japanese culture or media, didn't study a language for JC and are only taking up one now, or disliked whatever language you choose for JC.
